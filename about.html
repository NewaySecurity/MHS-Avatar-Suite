About Neway Security Training Academy

Leading provider of professional security training in South Africa

Our Story

Neway Security Training Academy is a division of Neway Security Services, a 100% Black-owned, PSIRA-registered company dedicated to providing world-class security training and education. Located in the heart of Mpumalanga, we serve communities across South Africa with innovative training solutions that combine traditional security expertise with cutting-edge technology.

Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to excel in the security industry. Through our comprehensive training programs, we prepare students for successful careers while contributing to a safer society.
