Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll through our website by visiting the Courses page and selecting your desired package. Alternatively, contact us directly for personalized assistance.

What are the payment options?

We accept bank transfers, cash deposits, and installment plans. Contact us to discuss payment arrangements that work for your budget.

How long does it take to complete a course?

Course duration varies from 1 week for individual grades to 5 weeks for the complete package. All courses are self-paced within the allocated timeframe.

Is the training PSIRA accredited?

Yes, all our training programs are fully accredited by PSIRA and meet industry standards for professional security training.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

Yes, we offer career support services including job placement assistance, resume building, and interview preparation for our graduates.

Can I study online?

Absolutely! Our platform is designed for online learning with AI avatar instructors, digital materials, and interactive assessments.
